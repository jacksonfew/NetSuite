Understanding and Navigating the Sales Order Detail Sections

  • Much of the information on the salesforce install case is the same on the new netsuite sales order and/or project
  • HOWEVER, the information is cleaned up into specific tabs related to your install on the sales order and project
  • In these tabs, you will find the information you need related to the install. The Same Tab section is also visible on the project for consistency.


ITEMS TAB  - Shows the BOM details and where production/assembly/shipping fulfill items


Property Overview-  Lists all the property details collected by the sales team

 

 Property Contact Information- Self explanatory, but can enter any contact information here. Also, pulled from the account




Video, Voice, Data Specific Questions – All the pertinent information related to the specific install (if applicable)





Shipping Section – All of the shipping related fields have been organized on it’s own tab



Communication Tab – Exactly what it sounds like, send emails to customers, internal co-workers and it all will be collected. Additionally, when the person responds, their response will be archived in this tab so everyone can see and follow correspondence. It will be used for all communication with customers and co-workers if the question is related to a specific project/installation/sales order
