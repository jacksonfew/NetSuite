Welcome

In 2020, Operations and Finance will move from SalesForce to NetSuite. The goal of this page is to simplify the transition by providing a resource that can guide you through the new processes in Netsuite.


If you are unable to find the answers to your questions in here, Oracle has provided extensive training documents which can be found here.


Additionally, there is the NetSuite HelpCenter that may be able to provide clarity.



However, if there is anything that is not covered here that you feel should be, feel free to submit a request here.
