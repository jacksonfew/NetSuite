Introduction

Air Freight shipments are completed when we have a requested to ship the item cargo through an airline, for a faster shipment.    


Air Freight Shipments:

United Air Lines:

Account # 8998334-0016 (ABP Digital, Inc.)

Sales number for the Raleigh area is 813-335-2971

  • First off you will need to place the booking either through email or via phone.

(you can call at 1-800-822-2746)

  •  Once that has been completed you can go to the airport and go to the cargo terminal.
  • You will have to have the MAWB (Master Air waybill, which they will provide you with the number once the booking has been completed.)
  • You will need the following below to place the booking:
  • Tender date
  • Tender time
  • PAL account
  • Total Pieces:
  • Total Weight:
  • Dimensions
  • Origin
  • Destination
  • Service Level
  • After you've turned all this in, they will proceed with shipping the product to the property.
  • All payments will need to be completed with CC at the cargo terminal or paid with over the phone.
  • If by chance it is an international shipment, then it would require the international paperwork as well
  • If any questions regarding this airline, they have plenty of information on the web page listed above.

Southwest Air Lines:

Account # 64084 (Allbridge, LLC.)

  • All shipments will have to be booked at the terminal itself.
  • You will need the following below to place the booking:
  • Tender date
  • Tender time
  • PAL account
  • Total Pieces:
  • Total Weight:
  • Dimensions
  • Origin
  • Destination
  • Service Level
  • Once that has been completed you can go to the airport and go to the cargo terminal.
  • Have a SWA Cargo Air waybill (if you don't have one, you can get one from the SWA cargo office (our agents there will help you fill it out)
  • Have a valid drivers license
  • Have your account number, we can NOT give that to you. 
  • You can either take a credit card, or they will bill ABP Digital for the shipment.
    • This is a reminder that Southwest Airlines Cargo DOES accept pallets within these guidelines.


1. Items affixed to a pallet shall be tendered as one piece.


2. Items must be adequately secured to prevent separation from the pallet and meet the requirements for weight and dimensions.


3. Items must be adequately protected to prevent damage to employees, aircraft and/or other cargo.


4. Pallet must be in good condition, free of broken or loose boards and have no protruding nails/screws.


  • For more information regarding shipments: http://www.swacargo.com/swacargo/shipping-HowToShip.shtml

  • To ship boxes, baggage or other items as cargo on a passenger airline, you must be approved as a Known Shipper by the US Government (TSA). This process is not available for individuals interested in a single shipment.
